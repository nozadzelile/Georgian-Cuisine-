About me

Hi! My name is Lile Nozadze, and I'm originally from Georgia. I've been living in Barcelona, Spain, for the past 10 years, but Georgia will always be my home. One of the things I miss most is the food. Georgian cuisine is incredibly rich, diverse, and full of unique flavors. I truly believe it is one of the most delicious and interesting cuisines in the world.

About this website

I created this website because I want more people to discover Georgian food, learn about our culinary traditions, and hopefully be inspired to try some of our amazing dishes. I hope you enjoy exploring the wonderful flavors of Georgia as much as I enjoy sharing them with you! 🇬🇪
